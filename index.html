How to Play Hangman 🎮

🎯 Objective: Guess the hidden word before the hangman falls apart!

Game Rules 📜

  • 🔤 Type letters on your keyboard to guess
  • ✅ Correct guesses reveal the letter in the word
  • ❌ Incorrect guesses make a limb fall off
  • 💀 After 6 incorrect guesses, you lose
  • 🏆 Guess the entire word to win!

Tips 💡

  • Start with common vowels (a, e, i, o, u)
  • Then try common consonants (t, n, s, r)
  • Pay attention to letter patterns

🎭 Fun Fact: This version of Hangman is unique! Instead of building the hangman with incorrect guesses, our hangman starts complete and falls apart piece by piece!
